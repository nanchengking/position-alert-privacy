PositionAlert Privacy Policy EN

Last Updated: May 9, 2026

1. Overview

PositionAlert (hereinafter referred to as "we") takes your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Core Commitment: PositionAlert does not collect any user personal data.

2. Information Collection

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

PositionAlert is a purely local-running application. We:

  • ❌ Do not collect personal identification information
  • ❌ Do not collect location information
  • ❌ Do not collect usage data
  • ❌ Do not collect device identifiers
  • ❌ Do not use cookies or any tracking technologies
  • ❌ Do not share any data with third parties

2.2 Locally Stored Data

The following data is stored only on your device and will not be uploaded to any server:

  • App settings (sensitivity, countdown duration, alarm sound selection)
  • Whether the onboarding has been completed

You can clear all local data by deleting the app.

3. App Permissions

PositionAlert requires the following permissions to function properly:

Permission Purpose Required
Motion & Fitness Detect if the phone is being moved Yes
Face ID Verify identity to disarm the alarm Yes
Microphone Record custom alarm sounds Optional

Notes:

  • Motion sensor data is only used when armed and is not stored or transmitted
  • Face ID data is securely managed by iOS; we cannot access your facial data
  • Microphone permission is only requested when using custom recording features

4. Third-Party Services

PositionAlert does not use any third-party services, including but not limited to:

  • No advertising SDKs
  • No analytics tools
  • No social media integrations
  • No payment services

5. Children's Privacy

PositionAlert is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of children's data collection.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any user data, there is no risk of data breach. All data is stored locally on your device.

7. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • View Data: All data is on your device
  • Delete Data: Delete the app to clear all data
  • Revoke Permissions: Revoke permissions anytime in iOS Settings

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Significant changes will be notified within the app. We recommend checking this page regularly.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:

  • Email: ezhangqiu@foxmail.com

10. Legal Basis

This privacy policy applies to global users and complies with the following regulations:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Personal Information Protection Law of the People's Republic of China
  • Apple App Store Review Guidelines

PositionAlert 隐私政策 中文

最后更新日期：2026年5月9日

1. 概述

PositionAlert（以下简称"我们"）非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明我们如何处理您的信息。

核心承诺：PositionAlert 不收集任何用户个人数据。

2. 信息收集

2.1 我们不收集的信息

PositionAlert 是一款纯本地运行的应用程序，我们：

  • ❌ 不收集个人身份信息
  • ❌ 不收集位置信息
  • ❌ 不收集使用数据
  • ❌ 不收集设备标识符
  • ❌ 不使用 cookies 或任何追踪技术
  • ❌ 不与任何第三方共享数据

2.2 本地存储的数据

以下数据仅存储在您的设备上，不会上传到任何服务器：

  • 应用设置（灵敏度、倒计时时长、警报音效选择）
  • 是否完成引导页

您可以通过删除应用来清除所有本地数据。

3. 应用权限

PositionAlert 需要以下权限才能正常工作：

权限 用途 是否必须
运动与健身 检测手机是否被移动 必须
Face ID 验证身份以解除警报 必须
麦克风 录制自定义警报声音 可选

说明：

  • 运动传感器数据仅在设防状态下使用，不会存储或传输
  • Face ID 数据由 iOS 系统安全管理，我们无法访问您的面容数据
  • 麦克风权限仅在您使用自定义录音功能时请求

4. 第三方服务

PositionAlert 不使用任何第三方服务，包括但不限于：

  • 无广告 SDK
  • 无分析工具
  • 无社交媒体集成
  • 无支付服务

5. 儿童隐私

PositionAlert 不针对 13 岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。由于我们不收集任何数据，因此不存在儿童数据收集的风险。

6. 数据安全

由于我们不收集或存储任何用户数据，因此不存在数据泄露的风险。所有数据都保存在您的本地设备上。

7. 您的权利

您对您的数据拥有完全控制权：

  • 查看数据：所有数据都在您的设备上
  • 删除数据：删除应用即可清除所有数据
  • 撤销权限：在 iOS 设置中随时撤销权限

8. 隐私政策变更

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。重大变更将在应用内通知您。建议您定期查看本页面。

9. 联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系我们：

  • 邮箱：ezhangqiu@foxmail.com

10. 法律依据

本隐私政策适用于全球用户，符合以下法规要求：

  • 《通用数据保护条例》(GDPR)
  • 《加州消费者隐私法》(CCPA)
